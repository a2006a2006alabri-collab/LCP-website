About LCP

We Build More Than Structures

Leading Circles Projects LLC was established in 2012 to focus on construction and engineering excellence in Oman. The company works with clients through trust, transparency, collaboration and flexibility to deliver successful project partnerships.

Our expertise includes residential and commercial buildings, industrial facilities, infrastructure, water and power works, reverse osmosis plants, sewage treatment plants and full MEP works.

At LCP, every project is a promise of quality, commitment and professional excellence. We aim to build long-term relationships by delivering projects safely, efficiently and to high standards.
